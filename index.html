A Techincal Mechanism In Polymer

This consists of a base polymer or blend of polymer (rubber) and neccessary property boosting additives which enables ease of processing and the ability to meet the service requirement

Types

  1. Base polymer e.g NR, SBR, NBR, IR, IIR, ABS, EPR, EPDM, all these can be used singularly or as a blend.
  2. Cross linking agent e.g Sulphur, Metal oxides, (zinc or magnesium). They are used for backbone chains that are unsaturated with pendant group attached.
  3. Sulphor donor: These are types of chemical sub-base substance that contains sulphur molecules.
  4. Peroxides: They are universal, which can be used for any rubber e.g dicumyl peroxides, radiation cure
  5. Polyfunctional amines: They are used for flouro carbon rubbers.
  6. Accelerators: They speed up the rate of chemical reaction but does not undergo composition. Examples are;
    MBTS, TMTD.

N/B: Accelerators increase the rate of cure.
