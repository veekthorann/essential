A Techincal Mechanism In Polymer

This consists of a base polymer or blend of polymer (rubber) and neccessary property boosting additives which enables ease of processing and the ability to meet the service requirement

Types

*Base polymer e.g NR, SBR, NBR, IR, IIR, ABS, EPR, EPDM, all these can be used singularly or as a blend.

*Cross linking agent e.g Sulphur, Metal oxides, (zinc or magnesium). They are used for backbone chains that are unsaturated with pendant group attached.

*Sulphor donor: These are types of chemical sub-base substance that contains sulphur molecules.

*Peroxides: They are universal, which can be used for any rubber e.g dicumyl peroxides, radiation cure

*Polyfunctional amines: They are used for flouro carbon rubbers.

*Accelerators: They speed up the rate of chemical reaction but does not undergo composition. Examples are;
MBTS, TMTD.

N/B: Accelerators increase the rate of cure.
