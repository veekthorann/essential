A Technical Mechanism In Polymer

This consists of a base polymer or blend of polymer (rubber) and necessary property boosting additives which enables ease of processing and the ability to meet the service requirement.

Types

  1. Base polymer e.g NR, SBR, NBR, IR, IIR, ABS, EPR, EPDM, all these can be used singularly or as a blend.
  2. Cross linking agent e.g Sulphur, Metal oxides (zinc or magnesium). They are used for backbone chains that are unsaturated with pendant group attached.
  3. Sulphur donor: These are types of chemical sub-base substance that contain sulphur molecules.
  4. Peroxides: Universal curing agents e.g dicumyl peroxides, radiation cure.
  5. Polyfunctional amines: Used for fluorocarbon rubbers.
  6. Accelerators: They speed up the rate of chemical reaction but do not undergo composition. Examples:
    MBTS, TMTD.

N/B: Accelerators increase the rate of cure.
